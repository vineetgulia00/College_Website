Facilities

hostels

The University provides residential accommodation on the campus to over 4000 students in its 19 hostels. Mess in each hostel is run by the residents on cooperative basis.

Medical Facilities

Medical consultation to Employees of M.D. University, Rothak. Medical Consultation investigations and dispensing of medicines for students. Coverage of various university sport events.

central animal house

Central Animal House, M.D. University is a central facility of the university. Research on small animals (Mice, Rat, Rabbit etc) is being conducted and animals are kept in air conditioned and calm environment. Institutional Animal Ethics Committee (IAEC), M.D. University is registered with CPCSEA, Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, New Delhi vide number 1767/GO/Re/S/14/CPCSEA dated 31/08/2017 for Research for Education purpose on small animals upto 31/08/2022.
